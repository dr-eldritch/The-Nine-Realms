Pteroi are a winged humanoid species who reside entirely below the tropopause, as they cannot handle the high temperatures and low pressures of the higher altitudes. They have complex societies which are based on their ability to variegate their iridescent moth-like wings; those with more vivid colours are considered more blessed, and are less often required to make the perilous journey towards the tropopause to porter goods to the Inflaroi. They are the diplomats and monarchs of the Pteroi. Those 'dullwings' with less colourful patterning are made to do the more arduous and dangerous jobs which support Pteroi society.
