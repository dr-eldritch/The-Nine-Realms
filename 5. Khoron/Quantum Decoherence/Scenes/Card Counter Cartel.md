The CCC are a secretive guild of 52 assassins who leave individual calling cards of four different colours at the scenes of their assassinations. Assassins may enter the organisation only when one dies or retires, and they begin at the lowest level - deuce. The council of Aces run the guild. All operations are performed completely anonymously to all except the Aces.

[[Ophelia Rose from the Snow]] was killed by the Black Jack. [[YOUnique Snow]] was killed by the Blue King. 

The contact point for the CCC is given as a QR code on the back of each calling card, leading to the nearest contact point.
