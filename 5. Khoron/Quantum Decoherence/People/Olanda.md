Olanda is Ophelia, Odessa and Olivia's 'mother'. She used her DNA to replicate Khori into a new generation of Bions which can self replicate. She theorises this but realises once Ophelia and You succeed.

She helped start Autarkis with You and Ophelia to keep an eye on them after she suspected their stealing of the information.

She has been working for the Vault all along, trying to further their goals to encode Khori minds and extend life. She wanted to implant Khori minds into Bions.
