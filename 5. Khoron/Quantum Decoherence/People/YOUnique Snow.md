Younique Snow (You) is the player character. 

Their wife [[Ophelia Rose from the Snow]] was murdered two months prior by a criminal cartel which had been hired by [[The Vault]]'s higher-ups, because she was on the trail of proof that the Vault's massive industry was nothing but a scam, that the tech to record Khori souls - [[psychography]] - was a fake. This would have destabilised the Vault and the class system of Bions below Khori, two founding pillars of [[YOUcorp]], the base sustaining power of Khori society.

She left You a cache of scientific papers which had been suppressed, studies for which the funding was pulled, algorithmic preferences in search engine code for new articles which display psychography more favourably - it was all there, if You could put it together.

You was also assassinated three months later when they started to put together the pieces of the investigation, this time by Blue King.
