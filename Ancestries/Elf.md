Elves are a humanoid species descended from the original colonisers of Luna. There are Glimmer elves, Shade elves and Umbra Elves; Glimmer elves live in bright golden fields and curious mind-bending forests on the Light Side of Luna; Umbra elves live under the canopies of massive dark trees which live on the Dark Side of Luna. Shade elves live on the borders between.

Glimmer Elves can teleport to anywhere they can see that is in sunlight.

Shade Elves can teleport to anywhere they can see that is in shade.

Umbra elves can teleport anywhere that is in complete darkness, and can see for miles in the dark.
