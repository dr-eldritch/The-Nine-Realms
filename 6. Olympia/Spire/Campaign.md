Begin in Smogtown and meet The Spider who gives them a task to be smuggled into the Avgrund and sabotage the arcane harvesters which power the Bifrost and fuel the navy, and more importantly Pontifex Arcanum. If successful, the city will be without transport except overground which will severely limit the Solar's capability to extend force over long distances. They will also find logs of the Pontifices' personal diaries in an ancient pressurised surface base. 
The party will then have to take out the Pontifex Arcanum (if there is time).

The second session will be a return to Parochia Obscuri to chaos. The Spider will then start to decrypt the diary entries, starting with Pontifex Umbra. She explains that they say the Pontifices can be defeated with this knowledge, but only once their sources of power have been defeated. She will entrust the PCs with knowledge to spread around the city, of the Pontifex's power source being ignorance, and work on decrypting the rest of the diary entries. The party will then have to take out the Pontifex Umbra (if there is time).

The third session they will discover the blood tithes are what keep Pontifex Populi in power. The PCs will have to infiltrate the factories and organise collective action against the Tolltakers. They will then have to take out the Pontifex Populi.

The fourth session will be deconstructing the power base of Pontifex Nemo. Since nobody knows who Nemo is, it will be necessary to take land and property by force and set up co-ops to run it. The party will then have to find and take out the Pontifex Nemo (if there is time).

The fifth and final session will be finding and destroying the contract of the ArchPontifex Stella and fighting her and any Pontifices left alive.

The PCs will start at level 5 and will level up at the end of every session / when they have destroyed a power base.
