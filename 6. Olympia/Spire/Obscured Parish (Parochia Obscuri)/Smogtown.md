Smogtown is just above the Immersea, where all the thick gases of Industry fall and settle. The people who live here are poor and of no standing, and celestials avoid this place. This doesn't mean it is safe to use magic, however, as there are always bounties and snitches waiting for a chances to claw their way out of Smogtown.

Key locations: Mama's Tavern, Happy Hog Butchers', The Bounty Board
