The Immersea is where the gases of Olympia get so thick they can be swum through. Many Kin lives in the Immersea. This includes some token celestials, although few, making it a relatively 'lawless' place according to the Solars. In actuality it has a complex social ecosystem and is safer than many places 'above' it on Spire's heirarchy.

Key Locations: Town Hall, Jovian Forum
