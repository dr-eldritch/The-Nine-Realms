Pavonis is the vast nation covering the North of the continent of Acordos. Its social structure is based on the fact that Acordians grow and mature much more quickly, reaching adulthood at 8 years old. This allows them to have vast generational families, which are matrilineal and matriarchal because of the control women are taught to have over conception. 

The land is thick rainforest in the north and rolling grasslands in the south. It has a cluster of mountains on the south border, wherein is located a Crypt. This is the source of Pavonis' superior technology and how they can stand up to the Syr Empire.

Piracy is seen as a legitimate enterprise in Pavonis, and pirates are often hired as bounty hunters or mercenaries in the war against the Syri.

The continent of Acordos is split into two countries and a DMZ. The other country is [[Arsia]].
