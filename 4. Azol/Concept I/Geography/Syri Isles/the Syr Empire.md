The Syri Empire is an empire beginning on the continent of Syria, spanning the [[the Independent Territories a.k.a. Noctis Isles]] and [[the Syr Territories]], and once owning [[Ascraeus]] and what are now known as [[the Independent Territories a.k.a. Noctis Isles]]. The latter two have been the subject of unrest ever since the Founding of Jani in 25 A.A..

They are currently in a war *via* the Syr Territories against [[Pavonis]].

Pirates are somewhat common in the Empire, despite the constant campaign against them from the Imperial Navy. The pickings are too fat for the pirates to ignore.
