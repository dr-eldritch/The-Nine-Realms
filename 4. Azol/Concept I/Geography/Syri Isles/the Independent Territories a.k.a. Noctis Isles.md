The ITs (natively known as the Noctis Isles) are a recently rebelled cluster of associated settlements on the Noctis Archipelago and the north-eastern coast of the continent Syria. They were able to rebel because workers found a Crypt and used the magic therein to fight back against their oppressors.

The ITs are a hotbed of pirate activity because of their poor naval strength, and because of the rich wealth and low security around the islands since the Empire left.
