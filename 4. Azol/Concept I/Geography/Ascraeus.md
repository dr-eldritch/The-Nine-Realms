Ascraeus is home to the native Ascraeans, also known as [[Draki]]. They were conquered by [[the Syr Empire]] and the islands were held for 80 years, until Ascraeus rebelled alongside the Independent Territories and declared independence 10 years ago.

Jana is a series of 3 volcanic islands, the large island Ascraeus (where the Syri capital Jani is located) and two smaller islands, Ceraunius and Elysium. All three islands are covered in lush tropical forest with a variety of unique biologies, rich in tropical fruit and spices. The Syr Empire spend decades ravaging the islands' natural beauty to export these valuable items.
