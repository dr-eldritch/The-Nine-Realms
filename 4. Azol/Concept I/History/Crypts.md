When the warming and sea level rise happened, many countries built Crypts to survive the hypercanes which were building in strength and number over the years. Crypts were vast underground bunkers filled with everything needed to tide over the high waters and winds.

Many Crypts were built in the high mountains and large igneous provinces and are now accessible by submarine as they are slightly submerged. Others were built high enough to still be on land. 

Three have been discovered so far, one on Criso, one on Ur, and one on Acordos. Many other unopened Crypts are rumoured or known about but are inaccessible.
