Many legends suggest that once Azol was a place of land and air, allowing humanity to spread and proliferate. Over time the gods of [[The Zodi]] grew angry with the hubris of humanity, who they felt were trying to become gods themselves, building towers to the sky and riding chariots of fire into the heavens themselves. So the god Siri sent great bolts of lightning down to smite them. However they forgot that sealed under the ground was a primordial named Ringwood, who was released and caused volcanoes to erupt and rain to fall for thousands of years, [[The Rain Age]]. Now, Azol is covered in water, and Ringwood's wrath remains in the hypercanes which ravage the ocean and islands alike.
