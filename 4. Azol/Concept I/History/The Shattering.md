The little we know of the Shattering comes from the most ancient of religious and sacred texts. Using fragments we can piece together the occurrence of an event, called the Shattering by many, before which there was but one mind, but after which were there many. More than that, we can do little but theorise based on scraps of information. 

It is proposed by some the Shattering was caused by an strike from the Heavens, though there are legends of a titan called Ringwood being responsible.
