The Miasma is the swarm of amoeba-carrying spores in the water, which can infect any who swim among them. The chances of infection when swimming among the Miasma is so high a mask must be worn to avoid infection. 

Once you can see it, it's already too late. 

When you are infected by the amoeba, you begin to express confused traits and increased longing for the water, eventually swimming into the Miasma and becoming a creature of [[the Green]].

The Miasma was created by a pre-Shattering biogeoengineering lab which was flooded when the planet's oceans rose
