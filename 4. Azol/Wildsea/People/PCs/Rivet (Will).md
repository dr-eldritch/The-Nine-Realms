Corron - Spit-Born - Rattlehand

Rivet (or RVT-7 to use his legal name) was born/built on one of the spits sticking out above the trees. He was raised to care for and to treasure machines. Machines are almost sacred to him, and technology from before the Verdancy is like a holy relic. He would happily endanger himself and the whole crew in order to recover lost technology, and would deem any loss of life as worthwhile even if the recovered item were a hairdryer or an electric toothbrush. (If you are familiar with them, think of a mix between Warhammers mechanicum and Fallouts Brotherhood) Rivet spends most of his time in the workshop or the engine room, both of which he considers as his domains (even if the crew disagrees). He isn’t emotionless, he is just logical to a fault. He has a soft spot for his current crew and is less likely to lead them to their deaths than his previous crews, but it’s not out of the question.
