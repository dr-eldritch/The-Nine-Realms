Auxite is a strange matte blue lightweight stone, usually containing metallic flecks. It is often mistaken for dark lapis lazuli. When placed against a resonator, gentle music sounds. It can be imbued with other so-called 'auxophoric' properties, such as universal attractor or donor of charge, magnetic moment or mass, proportional to the volume of the field created by the auxite's shape. Coils are therefore most powerful, but most difficult to manufacture. 

Practically, the few auxite artefacts in circulation are indestructible and non-reproducible. They are therefore extraordinarily valuable. 

The nine Ikons are the most powerful [[auxite]] artefacts, held by the eight Knights and the Monarch of Cydonia.
