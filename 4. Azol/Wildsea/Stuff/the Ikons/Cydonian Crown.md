The Cydonia Crown allows any of the other Ikons to be used by its wearer, and its genetically keyed to the royal line of Cydonia. It houses an artificial intelligence named Corona, who has since gone insane with nobody to talk to; since the Final Monarch perished without naming an official successor, the insane Crown is currently locked and can only whisper images of insanity and grandeur into the mind of any who wear it.
