Session 0: Thursday 16th. Sessions will be fortnightly thereafter. 

300 years ago, the Wildsea overtook the world, drowning the empires of old in a Verdant tide of overgrowth. Now the few remaining people scratch a living among the ever-shifting treetops and spits of solid ground. Some ride great chainsaw ships atop the canopy, others make their static living on the rare remaining islands. The people of the Verdant are multifaceted and deeply self-sufficient, coming in many shapes and sizes; from cactus-people to amber-preserved humans and fungus people to haunted driftwood. 

[Min 3, max 5] players may choose their own destiny upon the waves of the Verdant in this 'structured-sandbox' adventure. My GMing style is relaxed and player-agency-focused, which tesselates well with this narrative- and resource-driven game system with its roots (pun intended) firmly in 'Blades in the Dark'. 

This is a slightly different approach to RPGs compared with 5e, but don't let that scare you off! It's very newbie-friendly and the mechanics are easy to learn. Feel free to shoot me a message to ask any questions or join up!
