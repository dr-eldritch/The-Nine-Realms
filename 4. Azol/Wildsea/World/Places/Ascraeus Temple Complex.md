The secret main war hub for the [[Knights of Cydonia]], the Ascraeus temple complex now houses only the drained stasis-armour of the remaining knights, plus [[Asteri]].

# Entrance
The temple entrance is underground through a tunnel and into a large cave. The entrance's lintel/pediment is inhabited with pinwolves whose nest benefits from the consistent warmth of the neutral auxite and cover from the hypercanes.

The door is recessed and has a strange keyhole with many vanes. The disc is a fragment of the original charge acceptor and will get the door to accept the disc as 1 of 3 key fragments which must be collected to open it. 

# Antechamber
In the Antechamber are nine panels. Whoever has the grounder is pulled towards a scene:

* charge acceptor - scene of a flying arconaut gathering storms around themself

The five more scenes are of:
* age acceptor - a long time ago some Olympians were unhappy with their static society, left and named themselves and their new land on Azol on a plain they named Cydonia.
* strength donor - [[Cydonians]] leaving Olympia
* strength acceptor - Olympians arriving at (suspicious empty) Azol
* mass donor - [[The Crez]] throwing comets at Azol and it gaining its water
* mass acceptor - the scene is of the crezzerin generator at the South Pole of Azol drilling into the planet for resources and pumping out crezzerin
* * charge acceptor - scene of a flying arconaut gathering storms around themself then
* charge donor - the arconaut flying to the generator and being struck by an almighty lightning blast and destroying everything

* age donor - time passing and the wildsea burning to the ground, allowing for a glorious age of New Olympia

 After exploring the scenes, they will hear a whispering question: 

>Who are you? You are not Lunon; where is Lunon?

Asteri's voices seems to whisper from everywhere.
# Sanctum
When the Sanctum door opens, **play KNIGHTS OF CYDONIA**.

The sanctum has eight towering lapis-like statues of armour figures made of auxite. 

Lunon was Asteri's lover and closest ally. Lunon is the person Asteri gave the six remaining Ikons to, as he left during the ten-year hypercane that followed [[The Fulmination]] to recruit more Knights. He was to give them to worthy recruits. Only when six came together in agreement could they enter the inner sanctum.
