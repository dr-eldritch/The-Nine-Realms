"When knights of Cydonia
Ride before all
prophets and kingdoms
Will rise and fall."

Myth of an ancient knightly order of knights who rode Pegasi. Little else is known about them but everyone has a story of someone who knows someone who's seen the legendary Knights. 

The only remaining Knight is a newer convert, [[Asteri]]; driven to hide deep in the [[Ascraeus Temple Complex]] by the front of the approaching Wildsea and finding the ancient sanctum of Knights, Asteri studied the ancient texts and became herself a Knight of Cydonia, with the [[Youthful Chalice]] slowing her aging.

The nine Ikons are the most powerful [[auxite]] artefacts, held by the eight Knights and the Monarch of Cydonia.

- age acceptor - [[Youthful Chalice]]
* strength donor - [[Powerful Pauldron]]
* strength acceptor - [[Divisive Blade]]
* mass donor - [[Bounding Boots]]
* mass acceptor - [[Steady Belt]]
* charge donor - [[Fulminating Bolt]]
* charge acceptor - [[Absorbing Amulet]]
* age donor - [[Prophetic Tome]]

- command Ikon - [[Cydonian Crown]]

They must be studied carefully to be used properly, and use by an untrained individual is very dangerous. The Knights also had a rule against possessing multiple Ikons, and a taboo against even touching another's Ikon.
