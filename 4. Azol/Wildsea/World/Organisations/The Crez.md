The Crez are a set of species of insectoid humanoids who developed [[Crezzerin]] as a mass terraforming device. Their terraforming probe landed on Azol from [[Eden]] after they had bombarded it with comets to flood it. Only trouble was, other species got there first and colonised the flooded planet's three small continents and two large archipelagos, as well as its oceans. The millennia of comet bombardment drained all the powers of the protectors of Azol, the Knights of Cydonia. The crezzerin then had free reign to take over the planet, drinking its oceans as it did so, and turning into an insectoid's paradise.
