The characters sailed past a giant swirl of clouds and lightning, surrounding a tallshank. They decide to use the kitesail to collect the energy from the lightning into a battery Rivet has. This works remarkably well. The wires hummed discontentedly however. 

The sailors continue and use some of the battery's charge to boost themselves past a mysterious incident. 

However they were then caught up by the lightning from earlier, and had a tense standoff with a storm born. They wanted something that belonged to them, and Rizla chatted with them in the slim share of their common language. He managed to translate that the storm born was after his "pet/artefact". They realised he wanted, and so subsequently gave back, the charge from the battery. The storm born left. 

They reached a cliff edge protruding from a spit of land breaking up from the treeline where an earthquake had revealed hundreds of caves and tunnels. However the hypercanes was almost at its height, and they needed to get to shelter. The Long Shot crawled up to where madd addam said there was a big enough cave. 

They disembarked but saw blood and the trail let to a pinwolf feasting on a corpse. Kiri shot it with an arc rifle, then it skittered onto the ceiling and landed on Rivet. Teko finished it off. Madd Addam stayed far away from the corpse, not able to look at it. It turns out he knew the ex-person - it was his partner.
