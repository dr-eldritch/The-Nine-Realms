# Set

We will run for 3 hours, from 6:30pm to 9:30pm. I will begin promptly at half past six so if you are late you will miss out. We will endeavour to have a break from 8pm until 8:15pm or so. 

My main rule is be kind to one another and facilitate each other. This is a game of sharing the spotlight, allowing others their moment in the sun, knowing in doing so you strengthen everybody's experience.

Towards that end we'll have no acts of a prejudiced nature in-game, and certainly none of that above table. If we have a disagreement we discuss _above table_ and, if necessary, vote, with GM acting as tiebreak.

## Safety Tools

I will be using a safety tool called the _traffic light_ system: **red** is *do not show this in-game*; **amber** is *please don't describe this in detail*, though reference is acceptable; **green** is rarely used but if you have anything unusual you want to explore that would otherwise be avoided, such as _the experiences of you character with transphobia in their old community_, that can be discussed as a green light. 

Another safety tool we will use is the X card. At any point can anybody flip the X card over and we will move on, no questions asked.

## Setting
300 years ago the world was swallowed whole by an all-covering tide of trees powered by the mutagenic compound crezzerin. Most of civilisation was lost, and those who survived did so by being furiously self-sufficient. You are the crew of a ship of some kind; we will decide together what kind. Then you will decide where you fit on that crew, and where you're from.
