Teens camping in the woods. A meteorite lands. The teratomorph stalks them, gathering information, then attacks them one by one.

The players have a pool of 20 chips, and the GM gets 20 chips.

The players and GM blind bid to do things. If there's randomness, roll 1d6+bid. When playing it safe, roll Camp (below your value). When playing it risky, roll Fire (above your value).

Each PC has a power, roll Fire (2 to 5) to succeed on power, roll above to fail but gain instability. Effect die determines power/instability increase.

Available powers are: 
* mind control
* mind reading
* shapeshifting
* flight
* telekinesis
* teleportation
* super strength
* healing
* premonition
* impenetrable defence
