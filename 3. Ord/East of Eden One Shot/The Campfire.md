The Campfire is safe. The Campfire is home. We can see who is still themselves around the Campfire. 

Holding a teratomorph's hand in fire will not cause the same harm as with a human, so the reaction is not as instinctual. This can be seen by others around the fire.

Only one person can be tested per campfire meeting. Choosing who to test is a matter up to the campers. Whoever is tested loses a chip, GM loses one if the PCs are right, PC loses one if the PCs are wrong.

If the teratomorph is found out it will attack, giving them a chance to do damage to it. Damaging it 5 times will bring a final confrontation. After it is damaged it runs off and the replaced character returns from being lost in the woods.
