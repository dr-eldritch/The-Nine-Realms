The [[four Lifeweb torques]] do four separate things. 
1. Boosts your max HP by 5; next time you hit 0 HP your Forma die increases by 1. 
2. Boosts Mensa skills by 1 each; next time you discover something nobody else knows about, your Mensa die increases by 1. 
3. Can roll to attempt to read a creature's mind; next time you uncover a lie with an Insight check your Anima die increases by 1. 
4. Boosts your power regained by 1 every time you rest; next time you roll min or max on a check your Karma die increases by 1. 
They also connect the minds of the wearers.

When they are first put on they take a small blood sample *via* a retractable thorn on the inside.
