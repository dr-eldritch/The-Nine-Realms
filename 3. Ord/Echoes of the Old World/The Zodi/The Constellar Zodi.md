There are around 5000 recorded Constellar Zodi, each falling under the domain of a Cardinal Zodi.

However the expansion of the Deepness has swallowed many of these Zodi deities, rendering their magic inaccessible. Now only the Lodestar Siri is visible, her three sisters, Aki, Ingrid and Freya having been swallowed in the past few years. The church of Siri, the oldest church in memory, is currently in crisis as the Deepness approaches the star.
