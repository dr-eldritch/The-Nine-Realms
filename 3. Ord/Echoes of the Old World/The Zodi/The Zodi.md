The Zodi are one pantheon who live in the Heavens worshipped by around one third of the humanoid peoples across Ord. It is an almost exclusively Ordan religion, because the Zodi are nearly invisible from the other Planes.  

There are 4 major (cardinal) Zodi deities and 16 minor (constellar) deities. The Pole Star is known as Siri, the Lodestar, and represents galactic South. Galactic North is represented by The Deepness, an ever-expanding area of starlessness. Siri and the Deepness are not considered part of the Zodi, gaining the numbers 0 and XVII respectively in the [[tarakta deck]].
