
The door is closed.  
No light, no sound, no touch.  
The air thickens with absence,  
A weight that grows,  
A hunger that pulls.

Time unravels, frays,  
Everything that was falls silent,  
Flesh to dust,  
Breath to void,  
Memory fades to nothing.

Endings call, but none can hear.  
Shadows flicker in the dark,  
The ground trembles, but does not break.  
We are forgotten before we fall.  
We are the echo,  
The gap between.

Nothing remains but stillness,  
No eyes to see the fading light,  
No voice to call it home.  
The end was always here,  
Waiting in the quiet,  
Waiting in the numb.
