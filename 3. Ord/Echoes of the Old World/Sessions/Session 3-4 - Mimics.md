On approaching the castle/speaking to the inhabitants of Damf-upon-Mymet, it appears it is actually a monastery (a quantum mimic monastery). All the inhabitants & the very fabric of the building are quantum mimics, psychic beings which can detect the thoughts of any being nearby, and they become what the viewer expects. The mimics reveal that there is a monster deep in the bowels of the old castle dungeons, which they need the party to deal with, in exchange for which they can have a chest full of treasure.

The monster is a possessed mimic, a well-endowed woman, a very hard fight (30 HP, TN2 to hit/avoid attacks, 8 Power). It is contained in a very deep pit in the dungeon of the castle. It murmurs and continuously chants part of the [[Chant of the End]]. It changes form to appear as the party members, making it hard for them to determine who to fight.

Once the possessed mimic is defeated the party are led to the vault, in which is a golden chest full of coins. The chest is a mimic, one of the heart-mimics of the castle, and it tries to eat the party. They must escape the monastery without being eaten.

The monastery was once an actual castle overseeing the village mining operations.
