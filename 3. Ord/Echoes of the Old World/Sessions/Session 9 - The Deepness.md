Seven strange twists in space fly eternally above the crater that once was Invicta, buoyed up and fed by the waves of energy coming off it. From these twists pour unending 'plasmic entities'. Invicta was the archetypal Dragon Empire - a warlord dragon whose hoard was the military controlled its expansionist policy; a golden dragon controlled commerce and mining with control of the banks; now they all fly as living portals to the Deepness. The explosion and subsequent energy intensity has overwhelmed the sentience of these dragons who initiated the ritual, turning them into mind-wiped organisms, thus allowing them to be overtaken by the demons. This in turn let them use the power of the dragons to bring others through.

A Rising Tide (Raises All Boats), the dragon in charge of academies and education, was being manipulated by a mysterious entity from the Deepness to send its hoard across Antar to begin founding places of learning, promising the extent of its hoard was tied to the number of times they could be reborn. Promises of immortality clouded A Rising Tide's judgement, and he sold the plan to the majority of the 7 Dragon Councillors of Invicta. 

When it came through, it used the dragons' hoards to bring others through, burning through the hoardmembers' power to bridge the gap to the Deepness, using the power of the host as catalyst.

Now, 

Every 3 hours a wave of energy comes from the crater's core, increasing and decreasing in intensity like a sine wave to a maximum every 3 days and a very strong pulse every 3 months.

At the centre of these waves of chaotic energy is a hatch. Getting down there without being overrun or possessed is going to prove difficult.

When they get onto the mountainside they are almost immediately set upon by a group of survivors in the hills, who beat them senseless and bring them underground to try and 'cure' them of their possession; this, of course, does not work.

Depending on how this goes, they will eventually be sent by Gaius to try and open the Hatch, given they have shown some resistance to the mind-wiping effects. In truth, this will kill them almost immediately, if they try. If they die, they will be resurrected during the next maximum 3 days later. They will find themselves strung up outside the base.

Describe the constellation of souls, like a great spill of milk across the sky, and beyond them, the uncaring god-stars. A point of potential exists within the eternity of sensationless oily dark agony of the Deepness. Zooming in, there are pulsing points of bright energy in this whirl of chaotic potential, and the brightest of all is beside two others far from the concentrated knot of smaller, dimmer lights.
