Rotfall. Sleety rain starts to fall and the oilskins they are wearing begins to sizzle. Any exposed skin is rapidly aged. They hurry to the lee of a rock, where they are spotted by survivors in the area and brought underground. In a fallout-like vault people live, cowering under the Rotfall. They say a demon has taken up residence in the floating temple of Shi-La which is sacred to their people, and has twisted its impressive magic to generate Rotfall clouds. 

The people of the vault once called themselves the people of the Temple of Shi-La (Sacred Rain), which in times before rained refreshing, youth-giving showers at special festivals. Now they call themselves the people living under Shi-Ro (the Profane Rain, or Rotfall).

They will not even ask the party for help, not thinking the problem soluble. Nobody who has ascended the Thousand Steps has come back since the demon took over.

Should the party go and fight the demon (Azgul rented the place by the Vermillion), they will be rewarded with a drink from the fountain of youth at the centre of Shi-La. They will find the demon bathing in the fountain, polluting it and causing the Rotfall.
