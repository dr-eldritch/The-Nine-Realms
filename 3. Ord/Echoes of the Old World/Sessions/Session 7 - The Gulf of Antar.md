The Gulf of Antar is all that sits between the party and their goal. They need to find a larger 'Obelisk' barge, a hollowed out spear of stone which floats in the air. However, nobody is willing to cross the ocean due to an unspecified issue on the other side with the city of Invicta (the city is half crater now), as well as storms across the whole Gulf. 

On the way across the storms get worse and worse until they are forced to port at the isle of Berken at the edge of the Bay of Invicta. There they discover a community of possessed living in harmony with each other and the outside world.
