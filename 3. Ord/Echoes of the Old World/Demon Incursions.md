The Deepness has been encroaching on the sky for all of living memory. But now it approaches the last star in the sky, the Lodestar, Siri. 

But this year, on the holiday Sirimus which recognises the end of the year and the festival of the Lodestar, Siri finally fades and the Deepness claims the whole sky. And the world held it's breath.

Then all at once hell broke loose. The dead came from tears in the very fabric of veil between life and death to possess any they can, desperate for the sensation of life. 

This has occurred because of experiments by the Dragon Council in the fledgling empire of Invicta into pushing back the Deepness. They, in collaboration with a soul known as A Rising Tide, killed Ostra, giving A Rising Tide enough energy to push themselves into the land of the living. He then took over the Dragon of Education's body and used the mind as a bridge to the Deepness.
