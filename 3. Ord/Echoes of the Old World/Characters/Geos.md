Geos is the 100th leader who led the city to its downfall. Coming to power in a coup, Geos took over violently. They closed off the memory addition feature of the city's lifeweb, because they were misled with false evidence planted by an enemy state, that it was getting _less_ useful as more memories were added. They also wanted to stabilise the country and secure their leadership after the coup. Geos scrambled to correct their mistake, but it was too late. Confidence in the lifeweb plummeted, and confidence in leadership in general followed. This stagnation of the city's leadership caused economic and informational recession and the empire collapsed. Eventually people left the city to find new places to live. Over time Geos became the dominant set of opinions in the lifeweb, and has been in control since the height of the empire. He is very lonely.
