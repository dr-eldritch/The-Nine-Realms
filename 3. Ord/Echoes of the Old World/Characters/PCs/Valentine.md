Mother thinks she was impregnated by an 'angel' named Geos whom she had one night with while lost in one of the Old Places.

Actually she was already pregnant by then, the psionic effect of the 'angel' causing the child (Valentine) to manifest powers. If V meets Geos again he will discover his third parents' true intentions for implanting psionic powers into V. Geos is dying, and expects V to become his replacement. He will keep this from V until the last moment, however, hinting at it only. If V seems keen Geos will reveal all. 

Mother was a hunter-gatherer in the area around Brent with father before she became pregnant. When she conceived they decided to settle down in Brent to raise the child(ren) in a safer environment. 

Geos is an ancient artificial mind created from the mind-records of many, many old humans. They have impressive psionic powers but their age has rendered them immobile and their power is restricted to the Old Place in which they live. They can teach V many things but only if V agrees to take over the mantle of Geos, as many have done before him. V can choose to reduce the power of Geos in order to take it on the road, or stay for the full power, or promise to return later. Geos seems desperate and will try to trap the players if V disagrees.
