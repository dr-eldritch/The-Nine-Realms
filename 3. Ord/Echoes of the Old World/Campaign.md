Demons and monstrosities coming out of rips in spacetime. Discover that they are the permanently-dead coming back to life. This is happening because [[The Deepness]] is huge now, filling the sky almost entirely. Only the Lodestar, Siri, is now left as a star in the sky. And as that star too disappears, so too has the power brought by the god by which it is represented.

The Deepness has rendered the power of the church of Siri down and it is in crisis. And now the dead are returning to life. Something needs to be done about this.

As it turns out, the Deepness is Hell... And Hell is full.

>The world of Ord is old. Unfathomably old. Magic is in its very bones. There have been more empires than stars in the sky, visible now only in the archaeological and geological records. It is the home of humanity. 

> Remnants of these empires' achievements still litter the landscape, slowly being overtaken by the elements. Kingdoms and empires are broadly a thing of the past now, however, and those in Ord have discarded conquest in favour of a slower pace of life.

>You begin in the small farming town of Brent. Maybe you're fleeing someone - Brent is considered a backwater by most. Perhaps you've lived here your whole life, never having left the area. Or might you be seeking your fortune, having heard the rumours of a Crypt in the area? Whatever your reason, you are present for the festival of Sirimus, the last festival of the year before the sun dips below the horizon for Aetervek and the end of the long year. 

>Everyone has their place in Ord. As the gods watch over the sky, appearing as shining stars, the mortals tend the land. Ord is a quiet world. So it has been for a hundred generations...
