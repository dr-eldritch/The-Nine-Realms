Invicta is the capital of the Invictine Empire, the most recent and only current empire on Ord. It cover most of the Gulf of Rone, a sea trading empire, as well as having conquered much of the river folk, allowing them to spread into East Antar.

Invicta is based on a castle city, the castle built into the side of the mountain to protect and mine the Crypt found inside the mountain. 10 years ago the city underwent a socialist revolution, and in doing so ceased the campaign of conquest across the Gulf of Rone.

The socialist revolution was a workers' revolt against the ruling class of Invicta, the seven Pioneer Families - those who have gained power from 'mining' the Cryptss. Theseven families are:

1. Kromos - can change anythings' colour / hide
2. Klastos - can reshape anything / morph
3. Khrysos - can convert anything to gold / form
4. Kryos - can stop anything from moving / balance
5. Klavos - can open anything / learn
6. Kosmos - can join anything together / bind
7. Kaustos - can set anything alight / ruin

The power of the Pioneer families must be passed down intentionally from one member to another. Each family has different protocols for doing so, though they all require a specific catalyst object: an [[Oathband]].

The Party is the representatives of the Workers.
