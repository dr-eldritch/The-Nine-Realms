Ord is an ancient world. There have been many thousands of recorded fallen empires, and it is believed ten times that many went unrecorded, visible only in the archaeological and geological records.

The world is the home of humanity, in all its nearly unrecognisable forms. Magic lays thick over the land like weather, many forms and shapes of it from hundreds of near-godlike empires shaping the world to their whim. 

Yet they all fell. And life, well, life eventually slowed down. People aren't so hasty to kill each other now. Besides, it's hard to kill people now anyway.

I come from the old days, when the world seemed smaller and we filled it from pole to pole. Compared with the old days we are hardier but fewer in number. I feel society shifting to a slower tempo. But I have seen this before, and it never lasts. Some empire always comes and claims the land, scrubbing away the quiet space between people. Yet maybe, this time, it will be different.
