The Tarakta deck is a divinatory tool, religious focus, and chart of the prevailing pantheons and their power. It cannot tell the future, however, merely the present circumstances. 

The different cards represent powers and forces of the nine realms as they currently stand, ever-shifting and changing. The Minor Arcana are mortal powers, whereas the Major Arcana are the immortal powers.
