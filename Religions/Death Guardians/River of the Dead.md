The River of the Dead travels through the solar system from Sol to the Delta *via* the realms. Souls which die in the Hyperion System move to the river instantaneously, travelling outwards from Mercia to the Delta. Souls travel down the river, becoming increasingly difficult to resurrect, until they pass the Delta and into the Sea of Souls beyond the System. Most believe they are then lost forever.

The order in which the precincts are is determined by the positions of the planes at that time, with the soul slingshotting around Hyperion, passing through as many precincts as was within the orbit of the starting plane, before finally resting in the Delta.

The river is topologically different to the Nine Realms, being a straight line from Sol to the Deepness passing via every plane, with no visible light until the stars in the Sea of Souls above the Delta. The Realms are most often ordered, therefore, in the order that they would appear during a full syzygy.
